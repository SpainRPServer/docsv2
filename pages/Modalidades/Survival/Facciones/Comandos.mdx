💬 Todos los comandos comienzan con `/factions` (o el atajo `/f`).

⚠️ Solo se puede acceder a algunos comandos bajo ciertas condiciones, como no tener facción para `/factions join`, o ser líder o propietario para `/factions declare`.

## General

<details>
<summary>/factions create &lt;faction name&gt;</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Creates a faction with the given name.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;❔ Requirements: factionless

</details>

<details>
<summary>/factions join &lt;faction name&gt;</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Joins a faction with that name given it's open or you're invited.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;❔ Requirements: factionless

</details>

<details>
<summary>/factions leave</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Leaves your current faction, given that you're not the current owner.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;❔ Requirements: in a faction, not the owner

</details>

<details>
<summary>/factions info [&lt;faction&gt;]</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Displays your/another faction's description, members and power levels.


</details>

<details>
<summary>/factions list</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Displays faction members and power levels for every faction.

</details>

<details>
<summary>/factions map</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Displays a map in chat of nearby claims.

</details>

<details>
<summary>/factions disband</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Disbands your current faction, given that you're the owner.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;❔ Requirements: is owner

</details>

<details>
<summary>/factions safe</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Opens your faction's safe, if it's enabled within the config.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;❔ Requirements: in a faction

</details>

### User settings

<details>
<summary>/factions settings chat [global|faction|focus]</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Changes which chat you're currently sending and receiving from.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;⚠️ `global` sends to everyone on the server, and receives both `global` and `faction` chats.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;⚠️ `faction` sends to only your faction members, and receives both `global` and `faction` chats.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;⚠️ `focus` sends to only your faction members, and receives only your `faction` chat.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;❔ Permissions: `factions.settings.chat`

</details>

<details>
<summary>/factions settings radar</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Toggles displaying the faction of the chunk you're currently standing inside on the hud.

</details>

<details>
<summary>/factions settings sound [all|warnings|factions|none]</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Changes what type of sounds the mod will play.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;⚠️ `all` plays all sounds.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;⚠️ `warnings` only plays warnings sounds (eg. placing a block inside another factions claim).

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;⚠️ `faction` only plays faction event sounds (eg. faction losing power).

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;⚠️ `none` disables all the sounds.

</details>

## Faction Management

### Claiming

❔ Requirements: is commander

<details>
<summary>/factions claim list</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Lists all your faction claims.

</details>

<details>
<summary>/factions claim add [&lt;size&gt;] [force]</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Claims the chunk you're standing in, or a radius around it.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;⚠️ The `size` parameter is limited to a max of 7 (15x15, 225 chunks).

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;⚠️ The `force` parameter will ignore faction power and claim anyway, be careful!


</details>

<details>
<summary>/factions claim remove [&lt;size&gt;|all]</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Removes the claim on the chunk you're standing in, or a radius around it.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;⚠️ The `size` parameter is limited to a max of 7 (15x15, 225 chunks).

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;⚠️ The `all` parameter will remove all claims, be careful!

</details>

<details>
<summary>/factions claim auto</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Toggles automatically claiming every chunk you walk in.

</details>

### Home

<details>
<summary>/factions home</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Warps you to your designated faction home.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;❔ Permissions: `factions.home`

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;❔ Requirements: in a faction

</details>

<details>
<summary>/factions home set</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Sets your faction home to your current position.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;❔ Permissions: `factions.home.set`

</details>

<details>
<summary>/factions claim access [increase|decrease]</summary>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;💬 Change the faction rank needed to break blocks, etc. in that claim.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;❔ Permissions: `factions.claim.access`, `factions.claim.access.increase`, `factions.claim.access.decrease`

</details>

</details>
